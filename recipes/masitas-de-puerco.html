Masitas de Puerco

Description

This dish can be prepared in a myriad of ways. Latinos from many different countries cook this dish, but this recipe will be cooked using the Cuban style.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lbs boneless pork shoulder, trimmed of fat
  • 8-12 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sour orage juice (or 1/4 cup unsweetened orange juice and 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive olive
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 onion, sliced into rings
  • 1 lime, cut in wedges
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 bottle of mojo sauce

Steps

  1. Cut pork into 2-inch chunks.
  2. Prepare marinade:
  3. Mix garlic, onion, orange juice, 1/2 cup olive oil, salt, pepper, cumin, and oregano. Pour over pork chunks and marinate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Remove meat from marinade. Place marinade and meat into pressure cooker. If meat is not covered by marinade, pour in mojo into pressure cooker until meat is covered. Pressure cook for 45 minutes.
  5. Once done pressure cooking, remove meat and pat dry with paper towel. Heat up pan on medium heat and once heated, place meat into pan. Ensure the oil covers the meat at least halfway. Cook until lightly brown, then take meat out and pat dry to remove excess oil.
  6. Serve, pouring remaining marinade and/or mojo onto meat.
