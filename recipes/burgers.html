Burgers

Description

This is a straight forward dish to make with few ingredients. The preparation and cook time should be 10 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 Egg
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients into a bowl, with a few shakes of Worcestershire Sauce
  2. Make about 6 patties depending on the size burger that you prefer.
  3. Cook for about 3 minutes on each side or until brown on medium to medium-high heat.
  4. If you want cheese, add it on the last flip and cover the burger to melt the cheese.
  5. Put your preferred ingredients on your burger and enjoy!
